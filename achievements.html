Best in Everyone Award

National Computer Science Award

2x Nominations

In 2025, I was honoured to receive the National Computer Science Award as part of the United Learning Best in Everyone UK Awards. Having been nominated twice in previous years, winning this award—chosen from among students across hundreds of schools—was a significant milestone that recognised my commitment, skill, and enthusiasm for computer science. Attending the award ceremony in Oxford and connecting with other high-achieving students further inspired me to pursue the subject at a higher level and affirmed my passion for technology, innovation, and problem-solving.
